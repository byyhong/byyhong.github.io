Sights

Mount Rainier

Start early and watch the forecast. Parking fills up fast on clear summer weekends.

  1. Pack water and sunscreen
  2. Check trail status
  3. Beat the crowds by arriving before 8am

Bainbridge Ferry

Ride a Washington State Ferry schedule for a breezy daytrip across Elliott Bay.

Crossing time
~35 minutes
Cost
Passenger fare; pay in Seattle, return is free
Best time
Golden hour for photos

Seattle vistas never disappoint.
